Corporate Charter

OBJECT CLAUSE
IN A PROFESSIONAL FORMAT

The following section translates the company’s object clause into a clear website-ready presentation while preserving the full operating intent reflected in the source document.

A. Main objects to be pursued by the company.

01

Defence Systems and Strategic Security Technologies

To design, develop, manufacture, assemble, integrate, import, export, and deal in defence systems, arms, ammunition, drones, unmanned systems, surveillance systems, cybersecurity solutions, electronic warfare systems, and allied defence technologies for military, paramilitary, homeland security, and strategic applications.

02

Water Technologies and Sustainable Water Infrastructure

To carry on the business of water technologies, including water treatment, wastewater recycling, desalination, smart water management systems, IoT-based water monitoring, and sustainable water infrastructure for defence establishments, industries, municipalities, and rural sectors.

03

Agriculture and Agri-Tech Solutions

To undertake agriculture and agri-tech solutions, including precision farming, smart irrigation, drone-based agriculture services, agri-analytics, food processing, storage, supply chain, and value-added agri-products.

04

Deep Technology Development

To engage in deep technology development, including artificial intelligence, machine learning, robotics, autonomous systems, digital twins, blockchain, and advanced analytics for defence and civilian applications.

05

IoT-Enabled Infrastructure and Automation

To provide IoT-enabled solutions for infrastructure, utilities, defence operations, smart cities, environmental monitoring, and industrial automation.

06

Paints, Coatings, and Specialty Chemical Systems

To develop, manufacture, and trade in paints, coatings, and specialty chemicals, including protective coatings, waterproofing systems, corrosion-resistant coatings, and defence-grade materials.

07

Software Development, SaaS, and IT Services

To carry on the business of software development, SaaS platforms, IT services, cloud computing, enterprise solutions, data analytics, cybersecurity services, and digital transformation solutions including artificial intelligence and machine learning.

08

Renewable Energy and Distributed Power Systems

To engage in renewable energy solutions, including solar, wind, hybrid energy systems, energy storage systems, batteries, EV infrastructure, and microgrid development.

09

EPC, Consultancy, Audit, and Project Management

To provide integrated engineering, procurement, and construction services together with consultancy, audit, training, and project management across defence, infrastructure, water, energy, and technology sectors.

10

Dual-Use Technologies for Defence and Civilian Markets

To develop and supply dual-use technologies applicable to both defence and civilian markets, enabling broader commercialisation of strategic innovation.
